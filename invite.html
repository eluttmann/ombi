You're invited to Join Ombi

Ombi is a closed community where we help each other see unique places and experiences around SF. During COVID, we can help each other see unique restaurant experiences.

You’re part of a hand picked group of creators, explorers, seekers, photographers, thinkers and other adventurous types in SF.

The iOS app is live in the Mission.

This release lets you see unique restaurant experiences, hidden back patios and sidewalk beer gardens - just like if you were walking down Valencia.


What happens when I join?
- You become part of a group of creative, explorer types in SF. Soon you’ll be connected so you can talk with, learn from, get inspired by, collab with the rest of the community.
- We encourage you to share your experiences and what it feels like at the place with the rest of the community. You can do this by capturing video & photos via video pans, walkthroughs or put your own spin on it. Then, your videos will be added to the video story within the app.
